Basics of Sudoku Having trouble matching the letters? Try writing them vertically under each number.
Morse code is easier if you write down the sounds then solve. When you listen, try writing a dot for quick beep, underscore for long beep, and a forward slash for the space between the words.
For instance: * * _ * / ** / _ _ * _
In addition to filling in the spaces you know are filled, mark the spaces you know are empty with an X.
